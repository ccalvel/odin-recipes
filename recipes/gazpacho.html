Gazpacho

Description:

Gazpacho is a refreshing cold soup originating from Spain. It typically consists of ripe tomatoes, cucumbers, bell peppers, onions, garlic, olive oil, vinegar, and bread, all blended together until smooth. Often served chilled, gazpacho is a perfect dish for hot summer days, offering a burst of vibrant flavors and a cool, refreshing sensation.

Ingredients:

  • Ripe tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Bell peppers (usually green or red)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Bread (stale bread is often used)
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Water or tomato juice
  • Optional garnishes: diced cucumber, bell pepper, onion, croutons, or fresh herbs like parsley or cilantro
  • Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Prepare the ingredients: Wash and roughly chop tomatoes, cucumber, bell peppers, onion, and garlic. Tear the bread into pieces.
  2. Blend the vegetables: Combine chopped vegetables and torn bread in a blender or food processor. Blend until smooth.
  3. Season the soup: Drizzle in olive oil and red wine vinegar while blending. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Adjust consistency: Add water or tomato juice to achieve the desired consistency. Blend again until smooth.
  5. Chill: Transfer the gazpacho to a bowl or container and refrigerate for at least 1-2 hours, until well chilled.
  6. Serve: Before serving, taste and adjust seasoning if necessary. Ladle into bowls and garnish with diced cucumber, bell pepper, onion, croutons, or fresh herbs.

Enjoy! Serve cold and savor the refreshing flavors of gazpacho.
